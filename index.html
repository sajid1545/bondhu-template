    Jonas Tillberg, MPH
    Coordinator of International Programmes
    RFSU-The Swedish Association for Sexuality Education
    Stockholm, Sweden

    RFSU has always valued having BANDHU as a partner for sexual and reproductive health and rights in Bangladesh. Over the years, we have appreciated not only the strengths in analysis and methodology that BANDHU shows, but also the rights-based approach that is guiding the work. Together, these strengths and approaches have paved the way for the impressive results that the organization has achieved, and that are contributing to better lives for groups that traditionally have been marginalized and discriminated against. RFSU congratulates BANDHU on an ever-developing & successful work, and the rights-holders on having a voice-giver in BANDHU
